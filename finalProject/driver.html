How To Play

  1. Choose your difficulty and world
  2. If you'd like, choose the color of
    your cubes and floor with the RGB sliders
  3. Press Play Button
  4. Use WASD or Arrow Keys to Move
  5. Use Mouse to look side to side
  6. Right click to leave markers if needed
  7. Find the hidden Red sphere!
    8. Good luck!
    Note: To restart game, just press restart button
